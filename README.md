🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis (Python)

About

In this project, I analyzed Blinkit’s retail data to understand how sales vary across products, outlets, and locations.

The goal was to move beyond basic analysis and think like a data analyst—finding insights that can actually help in decision-making.


Key Highlights

* Total Sales: **$1.2M**
* 8,500+ records analyzed
* 1,500+ unique items


Insights

* Low fat products contribute ~65% of total sales
* Tier 3 cities generate the highest revenue
* Medium-sized outlets perform better than small and large ones
* Fruits, snacks, and household items are top-selling categories
* Older outlets show more consistent performance


Business Recommendations

* Focus expansion in Tier 3 cities
* Promote low fat products
* Invest in medium-sized outlets
* Optimize inventory for high-performing categories


Tools Used

Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn


What I Learned

This project helped me understand how to clean real-world data, analyze it, and convert it into meaningful business insights.
